My Story

My name is Reabetswe Dineo Dikgang, and baking has been a part of my life since high school. What started as a fun hobby gradually became a passion that brought joy to family and friends.

Today, that passion has grown into Sweet Cravings with Dineo, a home-based bakery focused on creating fresh, homemade treats using unique recipes and premium ingredients.

My goal is simple: to provide delicious baked goods that customers can always rely on for quality, consistency and taste.
